Welcome to CMSimple_XH

Congratulations on your new Language installation of CMSimple_XH.

You can login with the password of your main site. You will find the login link at the bottom of the page in the footer.

Please read about file permissions and security issues in the CMSimple_XH Wiki »

About the terms of license please have a look at www.cmsimple-xh.org »

There are 2 user manuals available:

  • English: www.cmsimple-xh.org »
  • German: www.cmsimple-xh.org »

The CMSimple_XH links

If you use an original or modified CMSimple_XH template, please leave the template-link at the bottom of the template. You may extend that link this way:

  • Template by CMSimple_XH modified by your link

If you use another template inside a CMSimple_XH installation, we kindly ask that you link back to www.cmsimple-xh.org in your template, since this is a nice way to popularize the CMSimple_XH–Project and of giving something back to the community.
